Woodborough · The Team

The hands
that know your dog.

A small, settled team — most of whom have been with us for years. Faces don't change much around the yard, and that's how we like it. Every guest is collected on a slip lead by someone they're going to recognise tomorrow, too.

I. Faces around the yard.

From the founders down to our most recent apprentice trainer — every dog you'll meet is in good hands.
