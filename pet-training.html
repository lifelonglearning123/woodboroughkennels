II. Our approach

Reward-based, results-driven.

We don't believe in compulsion-first training, and we don't believe in indulging unsafe behaviour. The path between the two — patient, clear, consistent reward — is what's worked for us for thirty-five years.

  • ·
    Tailored to the dog

    No two dogs need the same plan. We start with what your dog already does well, and build from there.

  • ·
    Owner handover

    Residential dogs go home with skills — and you with the handling to maintain them. We don't send dogs back without you.

  • ·
    Calm environment

    The walled garden is enclosed, distraction-controlled, and quiet. Ideal for genuine learning.
