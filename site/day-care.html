A day off,
well spent.

Four ways to do day care, all with the same off-lead garden access and the same family-run hands. Drop in for a school-run morning or book a focused stay-and-train day with one of our trainers.

Book a day All prices

I. Four options.

Pick the one that fits your dog and your day. We'll happily mix and match across the week.

— 01

Standard Day Care

A full day in our care — exercise, downtime, the works. Drop off and collect any time between 9 and 4.

£27.50 / day
— 02

Lakeside Walk

A long, varied walk down to the lake and back — water-loving dogs especially welcome.

£25 / walk
— 03

Adventure Pass

A flexible drop-off — for the days you just need an hour or two of exercise and supervision.

£15 / day
— 04

Stay & Train Day

Day care plus a focused training session — a productive way to chip away at recall, manners or reactivity.

£50 / day
II. How it works

Drop-off, done simply.

  • ·
    Reserve a day

    Email or call us with your preferred day(s). We cap numbers, so popular days fill quickly.

  • ·
    Drop off 9–4

    Arrive any time between nine and four — bring food if you'd like a midday meal served.

  • ·
    Vaccination card

    In-date DHP, Lepto and kennel cough — we'll keep a copy on file.

    Collection & delivery

    £30 each way within a Bath radius if you'd rather we collect.
