Woodborough · Cattery

A separate, quieter wing
for the cats.

Cats deserve a space free from canine commotion. Our cattery is in its own block — sunlit pens with snug hidden spots, unhurried handling, and the kind of stillness that helps a guest settle in by evening.

Check availability See pricing
I. The setup

Built around a cat's preferences, not ours.

  • 01
    Separate from the kennels

    An entirely different block — out of earshot of the dogs, with its own air, its own quiet.

  • 02
    Sunlit pens with hidden snugs

    Two-zone pens: a window-side perch for sunbathing, and a private retreat for shyer guests.

  • 03
    Family pens for siblings

    Cats from the same household can share — they tend to settle faster together.

  • 04
    Your cat's food & routine

    Whatever they eat at home, fed at the times they expect. Medication included at no extra cost.

  • 05
    Unhurried handling

    We don't push. New arrivals are given time and space — most are out and curious by day two.

II. Practicalities & pricing.

Cattery rates are quoted on enquiry — depending on whether you'd like a single or family pen and the duration of stay. Vaccinations required: F3 (or equivalent), in date.

Holiday season fills first

Christmas, Easter and the school summer holidays book up months in advance. Pencil in early.
