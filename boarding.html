I. What's included

Every guest, the same standard.

No upgrades, no upsells. Whatever your dog needs to feel at home with us, that's already part of the nightly rate.

  • 01
    Heated, individual kennels

    Insulated, scrubbed daily, with raised beds and your own bedding made up just so.

  • 02
    Twice-daily off-lead exercise

    Two long sessions across the secure walled-garden grass — the way dogs are meant to run.

  • 03
    Your dog's own diet

    Raw, dry kibble, wet, prescription, home-cooked. We weigh and serve to your written instructions.

  • 04
    Medication administered

    Tablets, drops, injections — at no extra charge. Vet liaison if needed during your stay.

  • 05
    Family kennels for siblings

    Two-dog and three-dog kennels for households who'd rather not be separated.

II. A day in the garden.

An unhurried daily rhythm that's barely changed in three decades — for the simple reason that dogs are creatures of routine, and so are we.

7.30Morning
Breakfast & first walk

Kennels opened, breakfast served to your specifications. First off-lead session in the walled garden.

10.00Mid-morning
Cleaning & cuddles

Kennels scrubbed and re-bedded while dogs are in the runs. Quiet handling time and any medication rounds.

12.30Midday
Reception walk-ins

Arrivals and departures welcomed. Reception is staffed 9–4, every day of the year.

3.00Afternoon
Second exercise session

Long off-lead run, second feed if applicable. Most boarders are pleasantly tired by now.

5.30Evening
Settle-down & final check

Final walk, lights down, individual goodnight check. The yard is quiet by six.

III. Before you book

A few practicalities.

  • ·
    Vaccinations

    We'll need to see an in-date vaccination card — DHP, Lepto, and kennel cough — at least two weeks before arrival.

  • ·
    What to bring

    Your dog's own food (weighed daily portions if you'd like), bedding if it helps them settle, any medication clearly labelled.

  • ·
    Drop-off & collection

    Anytime between 9am and 4pm. Dogs collected after 1pm are charged the night. Christmas Day, Boxing Day and New Year's Day are double rate and unavailable for collection.

  • ·
    Collection & delivery

    £30 per journey within a sensible Bath radius — chat to us about your address.
