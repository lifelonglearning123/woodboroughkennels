I. What we offer

From green dog
to deployable handler.

Whether you're a sole trader looking for a personal protection dog, or a business specifying a fully credentialled handler-and-dog team, we'll structure a programme around the outcome.

  • 01
    Protection & manwork

    Foundational bite work, escalation control, tactical recall — building from drives and
    capability.

  • 02
    Handler training

    Working knowledge that holds up in the field. Reading the dog, deploying the dog, debriefing the dog.

  • 03
    Bespoke residential programmes

    Live-in for the dog, scheduled handler attendance — useful when an off-site reset is required.

  • 04
    Maintenance & recertification

    Working dogs need working sessions. Ongoing support keeps standards where they need to be.

II. Standards & affiliations.

Working dogs are a serious business. We hold ourselves to standards that match the deployment.
